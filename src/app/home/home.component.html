Frequently asked questions

How do I access the forecast section?

Only managers have access to the forecast section. Employees do not have permission to view this feature.

Can I customize the KPIs displayed on my dashboard?

The KPIs displayed on the dashboard are predefined based on your role. Customization options are not available at this time.

How often is the sales data updated on the dashboard?

The sales data on the dashboard is updated in real-time to provide you with the most current information.
